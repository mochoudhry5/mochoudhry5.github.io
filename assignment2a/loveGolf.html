Love For Golf

In this paragraph, I will briefly talk about my journey to the game of golf. I remember when my mother introduced me to golf, I hated it. It was boring and really difficult to make friends as it is an individual sport. But as I started getting older, my mother started signing me up for golf tournaments and all of a sudden, I became addicted to the competition aspect of the game. When I was in these tournaments, I could not dependent on nobody else besides myself and this created pressure I loved.
